## Innovation Highlights Documented

1. **Ephemeral + Persistent Workspace Duality**
   - Ephemeral: Complete isolation, auto-cleanup, zero state leakage
   - Persistent: Multi-step workflows, mid-task handoffs, session continuity

2. **Model Tier Resource Allocation**
   - Flagship (Claude Opus): 2 vCPU, 4 GB RAM, 20 GB storage
   - Balanced (Codex, Gemini): 1 vCPU, 2 GB RAM, 10 GB storage
   - Fast (Aider, Grok): 0.5 vCPU, 1 GB RAM, 5 GB storage

3. **ULID-Based Dispatch IDs**
   - Time-ordered, lexicographically sortable
   - Natural chronological ordering in DynamoDB queries
   - Collision-resistant with millisecond precision

4. **Cost Event Decoupling**
   - EventBridge async emission (zero latency impact)
   - Enables flexible pricing models (subscription, per-task, hybrid)
   - Future-proofs for enterprise billing integrations

5. **Context Injection System**
   - Minimal (600 tokens): SOUL, JOURNAL
   - Standard (1,200 tokens): SOUL, ANCHORS, PROFILE, JOURNAL
   - Full (1,800 tokens): All + ROADMAP
